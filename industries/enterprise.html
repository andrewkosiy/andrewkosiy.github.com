Enterprise

Enterprises around the world are struggling to keep their assets under control and their inventory managed in their very own digital ecosystem. Leading global organizations are now able to leverage our data and insights to fully secure their assets inside and outside their perimeter, monitoring and reducing their exposure while eliminating both inhouse threats as well as vendors, partners or subsidiaries that are putting them at risk.

External Surface Management

Manage your organization’s global external surface, make sure both your visible and invisible perimeter is secured, and avoid security and configuration issues that may arise from an exposed asset in yours, your subsidiaries or third-party vendors’ possession.

Comprehensive Analysis

Explore your company’s managed and unmanaged assets and blind spots to minimize your global exposure and eliminate your attack surface. Look at the bigger picture or drill down and focus on a single asset to investigate and analyze possible threats.

Risk Insights & Prioritization

Identify your security posture, the exposure level, and existing security breaches of each of your assets before an attacker has a chance to do so
by getting risk alerts and prioritizing your internet-exposed assets
according to their risk level.

Inspect & Repair

Review your association to the vulnerable exposed assets you
identified in the system and get actionable remediation steps
instantly integrated into your security stack to resolve the issue
in no time and report the details.
