Insurance

Monitoring cyber insurance policyholders nowadays could be that much easier with a complete overview of their external attack surface. Insurance companies can now gain full visibility of security practices and policies of their policy holder as this growing market is becoming harder and harder to keep up with and insure, this is how you too can easily protect your company from insuring a risky business.

External Surface Management

Manage your organization’s global external surface, make sure both your visible and invisible perimeter is secured, and avoid security and configuration issues that may arise from an exposed asset in yours, your subsidiaries or third-party vendors’ possession.

Comprehensive Analysis

Explore your company’s managed and unmanaged assets and blind spots to minimize your global exposure and eliminate your attack surface. Look at the bigger picture or drill down and focus on a single asset to investigate and analyze possible threats.

Risk Insights & Prioritization

Identify your security posture, the exposure level, and existing security breaches of each of your assets before an attacker has a chance to do so by getting risk alerts and prioritizing your internet-exposed assets according to their risk level.

Inspect & Repair

Review your association to the vulnerable exposed assets you identified in the system and get actionable remediation steps instantly integrated into your security stack to resolve the issue in no time and report the details.
