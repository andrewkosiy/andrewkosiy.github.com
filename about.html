About us

Keep Calm and Manage Your Assets

Reposify was founded and built by a team of experts with broad, multidisciplinary experience in cybersecurity, cloud infrastructure, and software development, coming together to change the way organizations manage their assets and help them achieve cyber resilience.


Our deeper understanding of an attacker’s perspective and the risks in asset exposure, along with the Xray capabilities produced by our advanced technology and platform, allow us to zoom into any organization’s visible, or rather invisible, perimeter and take a close look at any of its associated assets, shedding light on many possible security and configuration issues.


Our mission is to aid organizations everywhere manage their external surface by reducing their exposure, eliminating their attack surface, putting the spotlight over their blind-spots, and providing security teams with practical tools and insights that will allow them to easily take actionable remediation steps in securing their external surface.


Reposify comes to bridge the gap between what assets companies think they own and what they really own; what type, what’s running on it and who’s allowed in - a vital new outlook on internet-exposed assets in today’s world. We believe that using our platform, any CISO or IT leader could fully master their organization’s external surface in a matter of minutes, with no installation or deployment required.
